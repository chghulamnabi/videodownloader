Encountering issues when downloading YouTube videos? This troubleshooting guide covers common problems and their solutions.

Common Issues & Solutions

Issue: "Video Not Found" or "Invalid URL"

Solutions:

  • Verify the URL is correct and complete
  • Check if video is private or deleted
  • Remove extra parameters from URL
  • Try copying URL again from YouTube

Issue: Slow Download Speeds

Solutions:

  • Close bandwidth-heavy applications
  • Use wired connection instead of Wi-Fi
  • Download during off-peak hours
  • Choose lower quality temporarily
  • Restart your router

Issue: Download Stops at 99%

Solutions:

  • Wait a few minutes - it may be processing
  • Clear browser cache and try again
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Try a different browser

Issue: Video Has No Audio

Solutions:

  • Ensure audio is enabled in player
  • Check if original video has audio
  • Download in MP4 format for audio+video
  • Update your media player

Issue: Audio and Video Out of Sync

Solutions:

  • Re-download the video
  • Use VLC player with sync adjustment
  • Choose MP4 format which merges audio properly

Issue: "Error: Failed to Fetch"

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable VPN/proxy temporarily
  • Clear browser cookies
  • Try incognito/private mode

Issue: Downloaded File Won't Play

Solutions:

  • Install VLC media player (plays all formats)
  • Check file isn't corrupted (size seems normal)
  • Update your media player
  • Try different player software

Prevention Tips

  • Keep browser updated
  • Maintain stable internet connection
  • Have sufficient storage space
  • Use recommended formats (MP4)
  • Clear cache regularly

When to Contact Support

If issues persist after trying these solutions:

  • Server may be experiencing issues
  • YouTube may have changed their API
  • Your ISP might be blocking downloads
