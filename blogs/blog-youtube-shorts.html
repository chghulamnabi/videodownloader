YouTube Shorts are vertical videos under 60 seconds that have taken the platform by storm. Downloading Shorts requires the same tools as regular videos, but with some considerations for the vertical format.

What Makes Shorts Different?

  • Vertical Format: 9:16 aspect ratio optimized for phones
  • Short Duration: Maximum 60 seconds
  • Different URL: youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID
  • Optimized Codecs: Usually VP9 or H.264

How to Download Shorts Using YTD

  1. Open YouTube Shorts in your browser
  2. Copy the URL from the address bar
  3. Paste into YTD Downloader
  4. Click "Get Video"
  5. Select your preferred quality (720p or 1080p recommended)
  6. Click to download - the vertical format is preserved!

Quality Options for Shorts

YouTube Shorts are available in several qualities:

  • 1080p: Best quality for sharing and re-uploading
  • 720p: Good balance of quality and file size
  • 480p: Suitable for quick previews
  • 360p: Minimum quality, smallest file size

Common Issues with Shorts Downloads

Rotation Problems

Sometimes downloaded Shorts appear horizontal. This is usually a player issue, not a download problem. The video metadata contains the correct orientation.

Audio Issues

Shorts heavily rely on audio (music, voiceovers). Ensure you're downloading with audio merged - YTD handles this automatically.

Best Practices

  • Download in highest quality available (usually 1080p)
  • Keep vertical orientation for mobile viewing
  • Use VLC or modern player that respects rotation metadata
  • Respect creator copyright and usage rights

File Sizes

Shorts are small files due to their short duration:

  • 1080p Short (60 seconds): 10-30 MB
  • 720p Short (60 seconds): 5-15 MB
  • 480p Short (60 seconds): 3-8 MB
